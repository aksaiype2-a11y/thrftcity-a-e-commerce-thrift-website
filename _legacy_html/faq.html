Frequently Asked Questions

Are the items authentic?

Yes! Every item is authenticated by our expert team before being listed. We guarantee the authenticity of every piece we sell.

How do you determine the condition?

We use a 5-point scale: Deadstock (New with tags), Like New, Excellent (minor wear), Good (visible wear but clean), and Fair (distressed aesthetic). Check the product description for details.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes, we ship to most countries. Check our Shipping Info page for rates and times.

How often do you add new pieces?

We drop new collections every Friday at 12 PM EST. Make sure to follow us on Instagram or join our newsletter to get early access!

What is your sizing guide?

Vintage sizing can be tricky. We provide exact measurements (chest, length, waist) for every item in its description. We recommend comparing these with a similar item you already own.
