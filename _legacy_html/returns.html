Returns Policy

At ThrftCity, we want you to love your pieces as much as we do. However, due to the nature of vintage and second-hand items, our return policy is slightly different from traditional retail.

14-Day Return Window

If an item doesn't fit or isn't what you expected, you can return it within 14 days of delivery for a full refund (minus shipping costs). All items must be in the same condition as when they were received.

Authenticity Guarantee

We take pride in our curation. If you suspect an item is not authentic, please contact us immediately. We offer a 100% money-back guarantee on authenticity.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email us at returns@thrftcity.com with your order number.
  2. We'll send you a return authorization and instructions.
  3. Pack the item securely and ship it back to us.
  4. Once received and inspected, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Note: Sale items and "Last Chance" pieces are final sale and cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or not as described.
